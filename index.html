El Loco Dino

This is the 2D Jump & Run story of Mina and her dinosaur park. The dinosaurs have been infected by a virus and have been attacking Mina and all her neighbors ever since. Help Mina to repel the invasion of the dinosaurs. The little dinosaurs can only be defeated with the help of her jumping power from above. The big boss dinosaur must be defeated with the salsa bottles and can only be attacked from the front. But be careful, there are not infinite bottles available!

Use the following buttons to help Mina on her journey against the big final dinosaur!

Walk Left: Key ARROW LEFT

Jump: Key Arrow UP

Throw a Salsa Bottle: Key D

Walk Right: Key ARROW RIGHT
